## 1. Executive Summary

PeteMart is a hyperlocal digital commerce marketplace targeting **5,000+ traditional physical merchants** across the **21 historic Pete markets of Old Bangalore** (Chickpet, Balepet, Mamulpet, Tharagpet, Cubbonpet, Avenue Road, Raja Market, Sultanpet, KR Market, Kumbarpete, SP Road, SJP Road, Huriopet, Basettyetpet, BVK Iyengar Road, Akkipete, RT Street, Kilari Road, Santhusapet, Cottonpet, Sowrastra Pet).

The marketing strategy is built on three foundational truths:
- **Merchants** (Ramesh persona, age 45-60) are low-tech, trust-driven, and need hand-holding. They respond to word-of-mouth, QR codes, and personal visits.
- **Consumers** (Priya persona, age 25-45) are already on WhatsApp, Instagram, and YouTube. They want authenticity, local discovery, and convenience.
- **The platform** has three modes (A: Direct Purchase, B: WhatsApp Enquiry, C: Visit Store) — each requires distinct marketing messaging.

**Marketing Budget (Year 1):** ₹12,00,000 total allocation  
**Primary Goal (POC Phase):** Onboard 50 paid merchants (Starter/Growth plans) and acquire 5,000 active buyers in the first 3 months.  
**North Star Metric:** 150 paid merchants by Month 6 (self-sustaining milestone).

## 4. Six Campaign Themes

### Campaign 1: "Discover Your Pete" (Launch Campaign — Month 1)
**Goal:** Create awareness about PeteMart and drive first 5,000 visitors  
**Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, On-Ground  
**Key Message:** "Your neighborhood market, now in your pocket"  
**Mechanics:**
- Instagram Reel series: 21 Reels, one per Pete market (fast-paced, music-driven, showcasing unique items)
- Facebook Event: "PeteMart Grand Launch — Virtual Market Tour" (Live on FB + YouTube)
- On-Ground: 100 QR code stickers distributed to pilot merchants; each QR code links to the merchant's PeteMart store
- YouTube: "Discover Your Pete" series — 21 short docs (3 min each), one per market
- **CTA:** Visit petemart.vercel.app and explore your Pete

### Campaign 2: "Zero Commission, Zero Tech" (Merchant Acquisition — Month 1-3)
**Goal:** Onboard 50 paid merchants  
**Platforms:** WhatsApp, Facebook Groups, On-Ground  
**Key Message:** "No commission on discovery mode. No tech skills needed. Just WhatsApp."  
**Mechanics:**
- On-Ground Activation: Field team visits 10 merchants/day in Chickpet + Balepet
- WhatsApp Broadcast: Send 2-min video testimonial from Samskruti Silks (pilot merchant) to 500+ merchant leads
- Facebook Group "Bangalore Vyapari Community": Post case studies, comparison tables (Amazon/Flipkart vs PeteMart)
- Referral Program: Existing merchants get ₹200 credit for each successful referral
- **CTA:** "Get your free QR code and store link in 10 minutes"

### Campaign 3: "Wedding Season Special" (Consumer — Aug-Oct peak)
**Goal:** Drive 500 orders in wedding season  
**Platforms:** Instagram, YouTube, WhatsApp  
**Key Message:** "Plan your wedding from 21 markets without leaving home"  
**Mechanics:**
- Instagram: "Wedding Shopping Guide: Silk (Chickpet) + Cards (Sultanpet) + Jewellery (Raja Market) + Sweets (Balepet)" — multi-store cart campaign
- YouTube: "Complete Bangalore Wedding Shopping Guide" (15-min video, 20K views target)
- WhatsApp Broadcast: Weekly curated wedding collections to opted-in buyers
- Influencer Collaboration: Partner with 3 Bangalore wedding/lifestyle influencers (10K-50K followers each)
- **CTA:** "Build your wedding cart on PeteMart — one checkout, 5 markets"

### Campaign 4: "B2B Bulk Connect" (B2B Wholesale — Ongoing)
**Goal:** Drive bulk wholesale enquiries via Mode B (WhatsApp)  
**Platforms:** WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn (light)  
**Key Message:** "Buy direct from Bangalore's wholesale hub — negotiate on WhatsApp"  
**Mechanics:**
- WhatsApp Catalog: Create a "Wholesale Deals" broadcast list for registered B2B buyers
- Facebook Group: "Bangalore Wholesale Buyers & Sellers" — connect resellers with Tharagpet/Mamulpet merchants
- YouTube: "B2B Buying Guide: How to save 30% on bulk purchases" (featuring Nandi Grains & Pulses Wholesale)
- Referral: B2B buyer referrals get ₹500 credit per successful reseller onboarded
- **CTA:** "Enquire on WhatsApp for bulk pricing — no registration needed"

### Campaign 5: "PeteMart Festival Calendar" (Year-Round Cultural Marketing)
**Goal:** Drive seasonal spikes across all 12 months  
**Platforms:** All channels  
**Key Message:** "Pete markets have a festival for every season"  
**Mechanics:**
- Content Calendar aligned with Indian festivals:
  - **Ugadi (Mar-Apr):** Holi colors from SP Road, sweets from Balepet
  - **Gowri/Ganesha (Aug-Sep):** Puja items from KR Market, idols from Kumbarpete
  - **Dussehra (Sep-Oct):** Silk sarees showcase, virtual fashion show on YouTube
  - **Diwali (Oct-Nov):** Lights from Basettyetpet, gifts from Kumbarpete, dry fruits from Mamulpet
  - **Christmas/New Year (Dec):** Bakery items from Pastry Cafe, party supplies
- Instagram Reels: Daily festive countdown with product picks
- WhatsApp Broadcast: Festival-specific offers from merchants
- **CTA:** "Shop [Festival] from the experts at PeteMart"

### Campaign 6: "Local Love Bangalore" (Community Building — Ongoing)
**Goal:** Build brand loyalty, user-generated content, and repeat purchases  
**Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, YouTube  
**Key Message:** "Keep Bangalore's markets alive — one purchase at a time"  
**Mechanics:**
- User-Generated Content: Encourage customers to share photos of their PeteMart purchases with #LocalLoveBLR
- Monthly Contest: Best customer photo or Reel wins ₹2,000 PeteMart shopping credit
- Instagram Takeover: Monthly merchant takeovers — a Silk merchant runs the PeteMart Instagram for a day
- YouTube Series: "Voices of the Pete" — mini-documentaries (5 min each) interviewing 3rd-generation merchants
- Facebook Polls: "Which Pete market is your favorite?" — drive community engagement
- **CTA:** "Tag us in your PeteMart haul — you might get featured!"

## 9. Risk Mitigation & Contingency

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Low merchant tech adoption | High | High | On-ground field team; "Mode B only" onboarding (WhatsApp only, zero tech) |
| Low consumer trust in online marketplace | Medium | High | Merchant video testimonials, "Visit Store" Mode C option, verified purchase badges |
| Festival season server overload | Medium | High | Load testing pre-festival; auto-scaling alerts; throttled marketing send during peak |
| Instagram/Facebook algorithm changes | Medium | Medium | Diversify across 4+ platforms; build WhatsApp as owned channel |
| Competitor (Amazon/Flipkart) promotion | Low | Medium | Focus on hyper-local UVP that big players cannot match |
| Budget overrun | Medium | Medium | Contingency reserve; real-time CPA monitoring; pause underperforming campaigns |
